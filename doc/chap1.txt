  [1X1.3 [33X[0;0YRepresentation in [5XGAP[105X[101X[1X[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YMost  of the time it should not be necessary to have in mind how objects are
  represented  in  [5XG2Comp[105X. An [13Xoriented edge[113X is a list [3X[e,s][103X where [3Xe[103X is an edge
  of  [3XK[103X  and  [3Xs[103X  is [22X1[122X or [22X-1[122X. An [13Xedge path[113X is represented as a list of oriented
  edges  (the  target  of  each edge has to be equal to the source of the next
  edge).[133X
